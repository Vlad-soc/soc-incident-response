# Incident: Phishing Email

## Incident Type
Social Engineering (Phishing)

## Detection
User reported a suspicious email containing a malicious link.

## Investigation
- Reviewed email headers
- Analyzed sender domain
- Checked URL reputation
- Verified no credential submission occurred

## Conclusion
The email was confirmed as a phishing attempt.

## Response Actions
- Email removed from mailbox
- Domain blocked
- User notified and advised
